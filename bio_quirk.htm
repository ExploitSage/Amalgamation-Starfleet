A.N.A.L. STARFLEET OFFICER RECORD

Last Revised:
Stardate 9045.22
Officer Name:
QUIRK, James Tyrone
DATE OF BIRTH:
6:9738.03
PLACE OF BIRTH:
Passaic, New Jersey, North American Federation, Earth
PARENT(S):
George Washington Quirk; Mamie Elenor Filmore

Quirk suffered head injury due to fall from a hovercraft at age two. Injury caused severe paranoia, acute homophobia, and delusions of godhood. After being released from ten years rehabilitation, Quirk showed himself to be proficient in leadership and was immediately drafted into Starfleet Officer Training School.

CONDENSED STARFLEET RECORD

GRADUATION: 4958.27, Third in Class.
Received H.P. Edsel Award for adaptation of WArP engines to true flight simulator. (Note: Quirk was penalized promotion for sixty days due to unauthorized removal of flight simulator without a flight plan or demolition permit. Upon appeal, he was additionally fined 500 credits for installation of engines without a building permit.)
4995.55 Helmsman, Ensign, DYF-0154  A.S.S. TITANIC
TITANIC suffered hull puncture due to collision with an asteroid when helmsman confused "port" with "starboard". Quirk was promoted to Lieutenant Commander for his uncanny leadership during the disaster. Credited with saving 300 of 421 crewmen by using the other 121 to plug the puncture. Quirk commended for suggesting use of new nautical terms, "left" and "right".
5000.15 Second Officer, Lt. Commander, DYF-0153  A.S.S. LUSITANIA
Quirk assumed command of the LUSITANIA when attacked by Klingfrees, guiding her to safely dispatch her survivors before her final destruction. Promotion to Commander.
5611.46 First Officer/Navigator, Commander, 3CP-5438  A.S.S. CHERNOBYL
Quirk was credited with finding theretofore unknown radiation pocket in Ural System. He also saved most of crewmen when CHERNOBYL was destroyed due to Rapid Argyle radiation leak in WArP drive. Promotion to Captain, 5995.73.
6001.95 Captain, commanding DYF-0151  A.S.S. BOOBYPRIZE
Promoted to Private Admiral for inventing BELCHON torpedo, 8601.95.
Following the apparent demise of his long-time friend and first mate, G. Spot, on stardate 8675.77, Quirk experienced a brief lapse of the mind-control techniques which had kept him in check. He led the BOOBYPRIZE on a mad chase through the galaxy which has resulted in the destruction of two races believed hostile to the Amalgamation, and the addition of seven other races to the Amalgamation.
Sanity restored, Quirk aids Rheal in putting down the B'thel'mi Insurrection of 9015.05. Promotion to Admiral.
"Keeping the peace as best we can."
