Our Intended Audience

This web site is intended for adult and near-adult audiences; parental discretion is encouraged for children and young teens. This site contains strong sexual innuendo, occasional profanity, and non-graphic violence, all in a humorous context. Oh, and plenty of funnies about the solid, liquid, and gaseous by-products of organic life. We offer equal-opportunity for the thin-skinned to be offended; politically correct we definitely ain't. Just know that the ethnic groups we poke the most fun at are represented in our own membership: Italians, Cajuns, Creoles, Germans, Jews, Negros, Yankees, and Rednecks. Usually each of us knows the best jokes about ourselves anyway, so why not share? Various weaknesses of the flesh, such as alcohol and prostitution, get coverage. There's even a bit of subtle bestiality innuendo, but we never mix sex and violence in a glorifying manner. SO, block us from the elementary and middle school kids, and find another site if you're easily offended. The rest of you, just laugh when you feel like it. And if you don't get the joke, try pronouncing that strangely spelled name. Just not in polite company.

Accessibility Statement
Notes about the navigation and other accessibility features of this website. This page is of use to anyone navigating the site using something other than a screen and pointer, or who has poor vision or pointer control, or who just wants to better understand the navigation bar or use the keyboard shortcuts. It is accessible from any page on the site from the navigation bar or through access key 0 (zero).
Site Map
A list of the pages on this web site, presented as nested lists to portray the site's tree layout. It also contains lists of this site's style sheets and images, and duplicates of all external links, sorted by the alt or title attribute, or file name if these are null.
