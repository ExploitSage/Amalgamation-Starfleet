Last Revised:
Stardate 9045.22
Officer Name:
OLHORA, Ijusta Nudder
DATE OF BIRTH:
6:9695.93
PLACE OF BIRTH:
Yomammahtu, Planet Ubangme
PARENT(S):
Yuneeda Laia Olhora; Yolanda Olphelia Washington

Olhora was born into the royal family of the Ubangme Tribe of the Ubangme System. Her position as third daughter dictated that she be sent away for education. While studying at the Jean Paul Duvalier University, Olhora's oldest sister, Pokatha, brought disgrace upon the family by having an illicit love affair with an Israeli doctor. Olhora joined Starfleet as a way to restore her family's position. Well-trained in displaying her charms and using them to her advantage, Olhora has also been schooled in 53 separate languages, 51 in which she is fluent. While in Starfleet training, she excelled in the use of communication devices, and was once considered for training in the Starfleet Diplomatic Corps, until she put a Voodoo curse on the entire population of the planet Cloroxtu, making them whiter and brighter than ever.
