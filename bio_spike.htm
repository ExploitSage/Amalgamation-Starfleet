A.N.A.L. STARFLEET OFFICER RECORD

Last Revised:
Stardate 9045.22
Officer Name:
SPIKE, Chrystaline Shaturzlikglas
DATE OF BIRTH:
6:7581.13
PLACE OF BIRTH:
Wahoo, Nebraska, NAFed, Earth
PARENT(S):
Getim Sicem Spike; Shirley Dissiszaef T. Glass

Spike's parents were famous members of a sect of non-practicing Druids (only because there are no trees in Nebraska.) Chrystaline was born at an auspicious time, seemingly destined for great things, but lapsing into eventual mediocrity. His parents immediately enrolled him in the Alexander Haig School's accelerated training program, culminating in his appointment to Starfleet Officer Training School.

CONDENSED STARFLEET RECORD

GRADUATION: 2600.31, Fourth in class.
Received the Neville Chamberlain award of Diplomacy when his graduate thesis of a possible peace treaty with the Klingfrees was immediately adopted by both sides.
Historical Note: Most latter-day historians attribute the outbreak of the TSING TAO Wars to the fact that this treaty had no substance whatever.
2618.39 Ensign, Navigator, WWF-3265 A.S.S. MAGNIFICENT MOULAH
 
3313.39 Ensign, Second Officer/Navigator, FIN-5678  A.S.S. MINNOW
Spike and a small team of Engineering Technicians modify the MINNOW's Super-Heterodyne Inertial Transponder, which enables greater accuracy in the ship's dead reckoning in space. After sucessful installation and initial testing, Spike was promoted to Commander, 3819.39.
Note: A.S.S. MINNOW disappeared 3884.39, all hands presumed lost.
4027.71 Ensign, First Officer, FIN-5420  A.S.S. SNAIL DARTER
SNAIL DARTER was surprised by an estimated eight Klingfree Battle Sheets during the TSING TAO Wars. Ship sustained moderate damage and managed to escape by using a series of unprecedented and unpredictable maneuvers, whilst and at the same time inflicting grevious damage on the enemy. Promoted to Captain and awarded the W.W. Corrigan Medal of Honor after treatment for epilepsy.
4804.98 Captain, Commanding DYF-0151  A.S.S. BOOBYPRIZE
Spike launches BOOBYPRIZE, and after first refit, begins exploration mission beyond the Deaseemarvelle System. After reporting results of assignment, BOOBYPRIZE recalled and begins border patrol duties, 5521.39.
Note: A.N.A.L. Diplomatic Corps sent beyond Deaseemarvelle System for indefinite period.
The "BOOBYPRIZE Incident." All details RESTRICTED.
Spike found guilty of insanity after destroying twenty-five A.N.A.L. ships of his own squadron, 5998.31.
Insanity diagnosis reconfirmed and begins treatment for Blue Marlin Syndrome, 8099.27.
8706.39 Captain, Commanding DYF-0160  A.S.S. ANDREA DORIA
After release from asylum, Spike begins recall of A.N.A.L. Diplomatic Corps from beyond Deaseemarvelle System.
Re-assigned to the Fantasmagoric Fifth Fleet in accordance with Operation Coronary.
"Keeping the peace as best we can."
