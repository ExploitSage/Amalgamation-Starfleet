DESOTO Class Battlecruisers


Artwork by John Slade

The DESOTO class Battlecruisers were designed by the galactic genius, Admiral Guhd E. Gumdrop. They are built with Trash WArP drive. They were designed to replace the EDSEL class Battlewagons, however, their huge cost precludes the building of more than five ships in any 7800-stardate period. They are armed with 10,000 Pharton torpedoes, 2 sets on Duzent Phazer Banks (member F.D.I.C.), and the experimental Wave Pool Guns, which have proven to be an extra defense to the Romulaid Croaking Devices.
