A.N.A.L. STARFLEET OFFICER RECORD

Last Revised:
Stardate 9126.10
Officer Name:
PEDESTRIAN, Red Cecil
DATE OF BIRTH:
7:0000.00
PLACE OF BIRTH:
Nike City, Shutoun, Payless System
PARENT(S):
Kross Walker Pedestrian; Mary Kay Reebok

Red is the only son of the son of the founder of Pedestrian, Hitt, & Rhrunn, financial consultants to the O'Bryan System, and of the sole heir of a large pedal cosmetics empire. His first claim to fame came as his father advanced him his first allowance of fifty cents, which he parlayed into a mega-billion dollar fortune within 39 stardates. He was able to compute compound depreciation of equipment before he could spell it.

Red was drafted into Starfleet by then-Amiga Sam Drucker, and put to work salvaging the economy of the Calcutta System. After founding the Bank of Calcutta and Credit Intergalactic (BCCI), Pedestrian completed his training at Starfleet Academy, where he excelled in improving methods of recovering war costs through the implementation of his Roberty Leasing System, by which the costs of projectiles are completely depreciated during their manufacture.
