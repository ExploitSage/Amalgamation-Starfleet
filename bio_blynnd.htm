A.N.A.L. STARFLEET OFFICER RECORD

Last Revised:
Stardate 9127.10
Officer Name:
BLYNND, Polly Esther (Leeszhursoot)
DATE OF BIRTH:
6018.12
PLACE OF BIRTH:
Froot O' de Lume, Washinware Planet, Dacronium System
PARENT(S):
Disk O. Leezhursoot; Cynthia Etic Phyburr
NEIGHBOR:
Ray D. Keelowaht
MARRIED:
Cottonn 'Fifty' Pursent Blynnd, 8009.39

Born at an early age to the unlikely union of a used car salesman, a lawyer, and a small appliance repairman, she became an enigma in her own time. While in grade school, she had trouble making friends because of her inherited traits, lying and overcharging. She overcame these problems and was voted "Girl Most Likely To" by her high school football team four years in a row. Upon entering puberty, Polly found out she had the ability to hear and to talk to inanimate objects. This enlightenment occured one day when she got her hand stuck in a "Pepsi" machine and she told it to let go and it did.

Later down the road, she had a brief stint as a jello wrestler, then tried a career as a psychic to the stars. This was short-lived when she found out that these celestial bodies resented this intrusion into their privacy.

Was briefly committed to the Harry Mudd home for the Harmless, 8144.39, after she claimed that her toilet told her to enter Starfleet Academy.
