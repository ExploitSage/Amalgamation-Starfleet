Officer Name:
FAUHTZE, Rip D.
DATE OF BIRTH:
7:0053.39
PLACE OF BIRTH:
Mobil, Exxon System
PARENT(S):
Smell D. Fauhtze; Lotza Burpz

Rip was born on the gaseous planet of Mobil in the Exxon System at a young age. She started working in the Mus-Turd gas mine early to help her parents. She soon passed by her peers to cut the cheese with the foreman. But soon her peaceful planet was attacked b the Klingfrees, who needed natural gas to fuel their weapons. She saved the planet by releasing, from time to time, large quantities of the planet's one natural resource. This release was modulated in imitation of the death cries of the male pit bull spider. Students of biology will recall that the male usually expires only upon forcible removal of his testes. The resulting din scared the Klingfrees the F*** off the planet. When Starfleet heard of this heroic action, she was offered a commission in the fleet, since they also needed the natural gas for their energy-delivering torpedoes.
